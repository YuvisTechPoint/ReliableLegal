Our Expertise

Areas of Advocacy That We Focus On

We work across key areas of Indian law, addressing sensitive and high-impact legal matters that affect individuals, families, and society at large.

Civil & Criminal Law

Legal aspects related to divorce, judicial separation, maintenance, child custody, domestic violence, and mutual consent proceedings under Indian family laws.

Cyber Crime & Digital Offences

Laws dealing with online fraud, cyber harassment, identity theft, data misuse, social media crimes, and offences under the Information Technology Act.

POCSO & Child Protection Laws

Legal provisions under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, focusing on child safety, reporting procedures, and legal safeguards.

Corporate & Startup Legal Services

Legal support for companies and startups including business incorporation, compliance, contracts, regulatory requirements, and risk management under Indian corporate laws.

Property & Land Laws

Matters related to property ownership, sale and purchase disputes, inheritance, partition, land records, and real estate regulations.

Medical & Healthcare Laws

Legal matters relating to medical negligence, patient rights, hospital compliance, Mediclaim, consent requirements, and regulatory provisions governing healthcare services in India.

Criminal Procedure & Legal Remedies

Understanding FIR registration, police procedures, investigation processes, bail provisions, and the rights of accused and victims under Indian law.

POSH Act & Sexual Offences

Advisory and legal support relating to the POSH Act, workplace sexual harassment matters, and other laws under the Indian legal framework.

Legal Consultancy & Advisory

Providing legal consultation and advisory services to help individuals understand their legal position, available remedies, procedural steps, and possible courses of action.

Drafting & Issuance of Legal Notices

Preparation and issuance of legal notices for civil, criminal, consumer, property, employment, and commercial matters, ensuring formal legal proceedings.

Rights, Duties & Legal Awareness

Awareness of constitutional rights, legal duties, and remedies available to citizens to ensure lawful protection and justice.

Animal Welfare & Environmental Protection

Legal aspects related to animal welfare, prevention of cruelty, environmental protection, pollution control, and compliance with applicable Indian environmental laws.
